Harmonia

A protocol for structured human-AI deliberation

What is it?

Harmonia is a formal protocol that structures dialogue between humans and AI systems through explicit reasoning cycles. Instead of free-form chat, every exchange follows a Perceive ‚Üí Synthesize ‚Üí Model ‚Üí Reflect loop that makes AI reasoning transparent and auditable.

Core idea: AI should show its reasoning, not just its conclusions.

How is it different?

Current AI assistants (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) operate as black boxes:

  • You ask ‚Üí they answer
  • No visibility into reasoning process
  • No structured way to challenge assumptions
  • Trust is implicit, not earned

Harmonia makes reasoning explicit:

  • Every response declares its sources and assumptions
  • Uncertainty is quantified, not hidden
  • Users can inspect and challenge any step
  • Trust is built through transparency

The PSMR Cycle

Each deliberation turn follows four phases:

  • Perceive ‚Äî What information is available? What context matters?
  • Synthesize ‚Äî How do these elements connect? What patterns emerge?
  • Model ‚Äî What hypotheses explain this? What predictions follow?
  • Reflect ‚Äî What confidence level? What assumptions? What's missing?
